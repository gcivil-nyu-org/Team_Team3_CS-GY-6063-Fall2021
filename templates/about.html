How to Join an Event

  1. Click on Events. Filter by Sport, Borough, or Event Date.
  2. Select events that look interesting, and click "Join Event" to join!
  3. Get excited to meet your squad!
Search for Events


How to Create an Event

  1. Select the Map for the sport you're interested in, to look for available facilities.
  2. Click on the map marker to see more detail about the facility.
  3. If you like the facility, click "Create an Event."
  4. Fill out the necessary information to get the event on the calendar.
  5. You'll be notified when your squad is formed!
